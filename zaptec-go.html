Zaptec Go highlights

Whether you're into the nitty-gritty details or prefer a set-and-forget approach, the Zaptec App turns your phone into an EV mission control centre.

The Zaptec Go is our domestic/home charging unit. The Zaptec Go has been through complete third-party laboratory tests, and it has been inspected by the Swedish Electrical Safety Agency (Elsäkerhetsvärket) without any technical remarks. The report from Swedish authorities is a public document and is available for download (PDF in Swedish).

Zaptec Go has:

  • Integrated RDC-DD approved according to IEC 62955. The four pole safety relay is mechanically coupled and meets the 500 A make and break and 10 000 A conditional short circuit current requirements.
  • Electrical safety according to IEC EN 61851-1 and other relevant standards. This has been tested and verified by a third-party laboratory.

Zaptec Go has an integrated RDC-DD which means that all Zaptec Go installations need a RCD Type A upstream. Usually, this is met by installing a normal low-cost RCBO Type A, which combines the MCB and RCD functions in the fuse cabinet.

No bigger than an iPad and only 1.3 kg. Skip the car compatibility check – our charger works with any EV.

Internet connection via WiFi with over-the-air updates for the latest features.

You can install up to three Zaptec Go chargers in each installation. Adding a second or third charger is an easy and quick fix.

Tap Eco Mode in the Zaptec app. Eco Mode tracks electricity prices to charge your car when prices are low. In addition, Zaptec Sense can collect total consumption data from your fuse box, in real time, so that you can set power limits.
